Our Services

We provide comprehensive one-on-one support tailored to meet the needs of each student.

Our services include:

  • Pre-college planning your academic and extracurricular pathway
  • Career and major exploration
  • Developing a personalized theme and strategy based on your interests and talents
  • Building a balanced college list
  • Interview preparation
  • Soft skill development
  • Time management skills
  • Essay writing for college applications and summer program applications
  • Application completion and submission

We also provide follow-up support for students who are deferred or waitlisted, offering strategic guidance to maximize their chances of success.

At Education Advisory Group, your dedicated counselor will provide personalized, expert guidance throughout the college planning process. Our services include tailored support in the following areas:

Pre-College Planning

  • Course Selection: Evaluate and recommend appropriate high school courses, including Advanced Placement (AP) and International Baccalaureate (IB) options to align with students' academic goals.
  • Extracurricular Planning: Strategize activities that highlight each student’s unique strengths and builds a compelling profile for college applications.
  • Community Engagement: Identify meaningful community service opportunities tailored to each student’s interests and values.
  • Summer Planning: Develop enriching summer experiences that align with students’ academic and personal goals, enhancing their overall profiles.

Testing

  • Admissions Testing Guidance: Keep families informed about evolving policies for standardized tests (e.g., SAT, ACT, TOEFL, IELTS) and test-optional colleges.
  • Test Selection & Strategy: Review testing options and set score benchmarks for targeted colleges and competitive majors, ensuring students’ efforts are focused.
  • Testing Calendar: Develop a personalized timeline to ensure proper preparation and effective time management.
  • English Proficiency: Advise non-native English speakers on IELTS or TOEFL requirements and strategies for achieving competitive scores.

College Selection

  • Criteria Development: Help students define essential factors for their college search, including academic interests, talents, and career aspirations.
  • College Comparison: Assist in evaluating college size, location, environment, and culture to determine the best fit for each student.
  • Exploration of Majors: Review potential academic programs and career pathways to align with students’ goals.
  • Targeted College List: Build a balanced college list comprising match, safety, and reach schools tailored to each student’s profile.
  • Demonstrated Interest: Provide strategic advice on effectively showcasing interest in target schools through visits, correspondence, and other means.
  • Exclusive Resources: Leverage our professional network to enhance the college search and application process, offering students unique opportunities.

Application Process

  • Timeline Management: Create a customized application schedule to ensure students stay organized and meet all deadlines.
  • Early Admission Strategy: Offer guidance on leveraging early decision and early action options to maximize admissions success.
  • Application Assistance: Support students in completing the Common Application and school-specific requirements.
  • Recommendation Letters: Advise students on securing impactful letters of recommendation.
  • Essay Guidance: Provide expert feedback on personal statements, supplemental essays, and other written components to help our students stand out.
  • Activity List & Additional Information: Optimize how students present their extracurricular activities and additional details in their applications.
  • Interview Preparation: Conduct mock interview sessions to boost students’ confidence and enhance their presentation skills.

Decision-Making

  • Offer Evaluation: Review and discuss all admissions decisions with students and families to determine the best fit and path forward.
  • Waitlist Strategy: Provide tailored advice and support for students navigating waitlist scenarios, maximizing their chances of admission.

With our proven expertise and a commitment to student success, Education Advisory Group ensures each student is well-prepared to achieve their college aspirations.

Meet Our Team

Annalee

Founder & President

Dr. Annalee is a trailblazer and trusted authority in the college counseling community, celebrated for her unwavering integrity, exceptional expertise, and transformative impact on students and families worldwide. With over 35 years of distinguished experience, she has been the guiding light for countless American and international families navigating the intricate and ever-changing landscape of college admissions. Her work is defined by wisdom, compassion, and an unshakable commitment to empowering students to achieve their dreams. As a leader in personal student counseling and parent education, Dr. Annalee approaches each family with a profound understanding of the critical connection between a child’s mental, emotional, and social well-being and their academic success. Her philosophy is rooted in the belief that every student deserves to feel valued, confident, and prepared to thrive in the next chapter of their educational journey. By fostering deep, trusting relationships, she provides students with the clarity, confidence, and resilience needed to succeed during this pivotal life transition.

Dr. Annalee’s global influence began early in her career, setting her apart as a pioneer in international college counseling. She was among the first American advisors to visit China, breaking new ground in supporting Chinese students pursuing education in the United States. Her unmatched cultural fluency and expertise in cross-cultural counseling have made her a trusted advisor to families across Asia and beyond.

In 2007, Dr. Annalee was appointed U.S. consultant to the Jiangsu Education Services for International Exchange (JESIE), an esteemed branch of the Jiangsu Provincial Department of Education dedicated to fostering international educational collaboration. By 2010, she became the exclusive U.S. advisor to New Oriental, China’s largest education company, where she revolutionized access to American higher education for thousands of students. She also facilitated unprecedented outreach, arranging visits for over 100 highly selective U.S. admissions officers to China, solidifying her reputation as a global connector and innovator. Before founding the Education Advisory Group, Dr. Annalee served as an independent college counselor, lending her expertise and mentoring over fifty college counselors working with international students.

She has personally guided over 1,000 families from the U.S., China, Canada, New Zealand, Myanmar, and India. Her students have gained admission to every top 200 U.S. college, while her mentorship has shaped the careers of over 60 college counselors, setting a new standard for excellence in the field.

Dr. Annalee’s groundbreaking contributions have earned her recognition at the highest levels. She is one of only 12 counselors to have served on the U.S. News & World Report College Counselors Advisory Board, and her leadership has been honored by esteemed institutions, including the University of North Carolina, Washington University, and St. Louis University, where she serves on their College Counselor Boards. As a Counselor Trainer and Supervisor for College Bound St. Louis, she continues to expand access to life-changing educational opportunities for underserved communities.

Dr. Annalee’s unparalleled legacy is one of innovation, impact, and an unrelenting dedication to student success. With her unmatched expertise and heartfelt commitment to fostering confidence, purpose, and opportunity, she has empowered families across the globe to turn their educational aspirations into reality.
